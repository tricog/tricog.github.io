FOR PATIENTS
1
Search for your nearest Tricog clinic in case of chest pain.
2
Select the clinic and inform the doctor of your arrival.
3
The doctor checks your ECG using a Tricog device.
4
Receive the ECG diagnosis in a simplified form on your phone.
5
The doctor takes immediate action in case of an abnormality.
6
Track your diagnosis and progress of your recovery anytime, anywhere.
FOR DOCTORS
1
Get prior notification of the patient’s arrival and track their ETA to schedule the check-up.
2
Connect them to a Tricog device and get instant access to their medical history.
3
Get detailed ECG diagnosis along with details of their state of health.
4
In case of an abnormality, access tertiary care facilities in the vicinity.
5
Contact the cardiologist at the nearest hospital who gets immediately notified of the patient’s arrival and ETA.
